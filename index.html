Revolutionize your blood sugar management

SugarScope is a noninvasive, light-based glucose monitor that helps people track their blood sugar and take control of their health.

Join our Newsletter Learn More ➤

Over 850 million people worldwide live with diabetes, a chronic condition that requires regular blood glucose monitoring, usually through needle-based finger prick tests or continuous glucose monitors. For many diabetics, the discomfort, inconvenience, and high cost of these invasive monitoring methods discourage consistent management, leading to avoidable crises and emergencies.

SugarScope is here to change that.

Easy to use

Just place the device on your finger and power it on. SugarScope automatically captures your glucose reading. No setup, calibration, or training required.

Simple tracking

View your glucose trends in real time through the mobile app. Track patterns over hours or days to better understand how your body responds to meals, activity, and more.

No needles

Ditch the needles! Experience a hassle-free way to monitor your glucose with our state-of-the-art optical sensor. Take charge, effortlessly.
